## What to keep

- The same-day, two-specific-times ask — genuinely the model ask of the batch.
- The symptom-broadening discovery (pain → energy/gut/anxiety) that makes the nervous-system pitch land for an adult skeptic.

## Leak points to fix

1. **The second-ask cave is the coaching moment.** She knows the deflection — she ran it 90 seconds earlier. Drill the "pushed-again" line from the scorecard verbatim: *"I really get why you want a number — I'd be guessing, and I respect your decision too much to do that."* Then two times, again.
2. **Never un-book a booked call.** The spouse move is: keep the slot, make the deposit the no-risk holder ("it's $67, fully applied, and if he says no we refund it — but 4:15 today is yours"). At minimum: "I'll hold 4:15 until 2 o'clock and text you." Pulling the appointment *for* the prospect is training the whole pipeline to leak at the finish line.
3. **Set a specific callback time when a call-back is unavoidable.** "Call us whenever" is the rubric's −10 for a reason.
