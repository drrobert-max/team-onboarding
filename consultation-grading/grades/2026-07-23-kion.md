## ⚠️ Out-of-rubric compliance flag (for the owner, not scored)

Recommend a clear script boundary: empathize with the parent's experience without affirming medical causation, e.g. "I hear you, and the doctors will look at his whole history at the exam." This needs a direct coaching conversation regardless of the sales rubric.
