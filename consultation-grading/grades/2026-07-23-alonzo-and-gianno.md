## Leak points to fix

She had a yes; she needed a calendar, not a callback.
2. **Don't run a two-child consult with a driving parent.** Offer a 30-minute callback *at the start* — the whole close depended on a wallet she couldn't reach.
3. **Same price-script replacement as Ryker/Kion.**
4. **Follow-up check for the owner:** verify whether the 20–30-minute callback happened and whether the boys got booked; that outcome belongs next to this grade in the tracker.
